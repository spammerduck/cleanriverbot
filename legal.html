Liability & Safety

CLEANRIVER BOT units are equipped with multiple failsafe protocols. However, aquatic environments are inherently volatile.

Safety Protocol A-1

Immediate propulsion shutdown upon detection of biological movement within 2 meters.

Safety Protocol B-4

Visual and auditory distress signals activation during low-energy or mechanical failure states.
