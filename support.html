Field Deployment FAQ

How do units handle extreme water turbulence? expand_more
Each unit is equipped with dynamic ballast control and high-torque dual thrusters. In the event of heavy flooding or turbulence above 4 knots, the bot automatically engages "Anchor Mode" to maintain its position until conditions stabilize.
What is the standard maintenance cycle? expand_more
Physical inspections are recommended every 180 days. The internal diagnostic system performs self-checks every 6 hours and flags any degradation in sensor precision or motor efficiency.
Can multiple bots communicate in a single sector? expand_more
Yes. The CleanRiver Bot uses a mesh network protocol. Units within 5km of each other share real-time sonar data to prevent collisions and optimize garbage collection coverage patterns.
